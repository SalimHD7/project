About

History

Mario, the beloved plumber and iconic mascot of Nintendo, made his debut in 1981 in the arcade game "Donkey Kong." Created by designer Shigeru Miyamoto, Mario was originally known as "Jumpman." His mission was to rescue a damsel in distress, Princess Peach, from the clutches of the giant ape, Donkey Kong. This simple premise marked the beginning of a legendary franchise. Over the years, Mario has evolved from a pixelated character into a fully realized hero, starring in a wide variety of games across numerous genres, including platformers, racing, sports, and RPGs. The introduction of "Super Mario Bros." in 1985 revolutionized the gaming industry, establishing the platformer genre and introducing gameplay mechanics that are still influential today.

Key Games

Super Mario Bros. (1985): The game that launched a franchise, featuring Mario's quest to save Princess Peach from Bowser. Super Mario 64 (1996): A groundbreaking 3D platformer that expanded Mario’s world and gameplay mechanics, allowing players to explore Princess Peach’s castle. Super Mario Galaxy (2007): A critically acclaimed title that introduced gravity-defying mechanics and stunning visuals set in outer space. Mario Kart Series: First released in 1992, this racing franchise allows players to race against each other using familiar characters and items from the Mario universe. Super Mario Odyssey (2017): A 3D adventure that takes Mario around the globe, introducing new gameplay elements such as capturing enemies using Cappy. Cultural Impact Mario is more than just a video game character; he has become a cultural icon recognized worldwide. He has appeared in numerous spin-off games, animated series, merchandise, and even a feature film. Mario's cheerful demeanor, catchy theme music, and adventurous spirit have made him a beloved figure for gamers of all ages.

Games

Super Mario Bros.

Release Date: September 13, 1985.
Join Mario in this iconic platformer as he embarks on a quest to rescue Princess Peach from Bowser. Jump through colorful worlds, defeat enemies, and collect power-ups to save the Mushroom Kingdom!

Super Mario World

Release Date: November 21, 1990.
Description: In this classic, Mario and Luigi explore Dinosaur Land to rescue Princess Toadstool. Featuring Yoshi, the game introduced new abilities, secrets, and vibrant graphics, expanding the beloved gameplay.

Super Mario 64

Release Date: June 23, 1996.
Mario takes a leap into 3D in this groundbreaking platformer. Explore Princess Peach's castle, collect Power Stars, and navigate whimsical worlds filled with unique challenges and innovative gameplay.

Super Mario Galaxy 2

Release Date: May 23, 2010
Embark on a cosmic journey with Mario as he travels across diverse galaxies. This sequel enhances gameplay with Yoshi, new power-ups, and creative level designs, delivering an unforgettable space adventure!

Super Mario 3D Land

Release Date: November 3, 2011.
Combining 3D platforming with classic elements, Mario explores imaginative levels filled with creative challenges. Utilize the Super Leaf to glide and navigate through this fun-filled adventure on the 3DS!

Super Mario 3D World

Release Date: November 21, 2013.
Mario and friends unite in this cooperative platformer. Explore imaginative levels, use power-ups like the Cat Suit, and tackle challenges together in a vibrant, 3D world brimming with fun and excitement!.

Charachters

Mario

The main hero of the Mushroom Kingdom. Mario is always bright and cheerful and instantly recognizable with his blue overalls, red cap, and trademark moustache.

Luigi

Mario's brother and fellow hero of the Mushroom Kingdom. Luigi is instantly recognizable in his trademark green hat and green shirt.

Toad

A resident of the Mushroom Kingdom who works in service of Princess Peach. Toad has red spots on his head, though others of his kind come in a variety of colors.

Yoshi

Mario's dependable companion who hails from Yoshi's Island. He is green, but others of his kind may be other colors such as red, blue, pink, and yellow.

Waluigi

Wario's pal and accomplice. Waluigi is the self-proclaimed rival of Luigi. Waluigi is willing to put in a lot of effort to best Mario and Luigi, if only to annoy them

Wario

The self-professed archrival of Mario. Wario wears purple overalls, a yellow hat, and has an instantly recognizable zigzag moustache.

Bowser

The King of the Koopas. Bowser is Mario's archrival and is always causing trouble in the Mushroom Kingdom.

Princess Peach

The beloved princess of the Mushroom Kingdom. She's extremely kind and is always working to create a world where everyone can live together happily.

Daisy

The princess of Sarasaland. Her trademark style includes her yellow dress and flowery accessories.
